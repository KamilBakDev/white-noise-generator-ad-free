Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 7, 2026

White Noise Generator Ad-Free ("the App") is developed by Kamil Bak. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All audio is generated locally on your device. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is used.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. It works entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools.

Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to continue playing audio when the app is in the background.
  • WAKE_LOCK — to prevent the device from sleeping during audio playback.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS — to control audio output settings.

No microphone access or recording permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
