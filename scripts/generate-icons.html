3. Replace assets/images/splash.png with downloaded splash.png

5. Upload feature-graphic.png to Play Console → Store listing → Feature graphic
6. Upload play-store-icon.png to Play Console → Store listing → App icon (512x512)
7. Rebuild: npx eas-cli build --platform android --profile production
